About Us

Welcome to Sri Foods

Welcome to Sri Foods, where passion for food meets a delightful dining experience. Nestled in the heart of Chennai (Velacherry), our culinary journey began with a simple goal: to create unforgettable moments through exceptional flavors.

At Sri Foods, we believe in more than just serving delicious dishes; we strive to curate an atmosphere that feels like an extension of your own dining room—a place where laughter echoes, and every bite tells a story.

Thank you for being a part of our journey. Here's to good food, great company, and memories that linger long after the last bite."
